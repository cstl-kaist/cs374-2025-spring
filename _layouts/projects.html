What do we do?

CS374 has a short, team-based "Mini-Project" in the beginning of the semester as well as a longer team-based design project, in which you'll work through the full user-centered design process from needfinding up through a high-fidelity prototype and user testing. You'll work in teams of three to four people. More information about each milestone will be added to this page as the assignments are released.

  • Mini-Project (due week 4)
Please note that all team members will receive the same grade for all the milestones, unless there are contribution issues identified.

Late Policy

For each milestone deadline, all members of your team will lose 10% for each late day. Submissions will be accepted until three days after the deadline. After then you'll get 0 on that assignment. Please note that late submissions for final presentations are not allowed.
